               SIP MICROPHONE TEST

This test allows you to record and play back sound
using the #YSIP microphone#Y.

Press #YX#Y to record a sound and #CA#C to play it back.

You can change the number of seconds to record
with the #YD-pad#Y.

The #YY#Y button toggles amplification during
playback.

The test auto-detects the microphone sample rate,
and you can change between 8/11khz with the 
#YR trigger#Y.
