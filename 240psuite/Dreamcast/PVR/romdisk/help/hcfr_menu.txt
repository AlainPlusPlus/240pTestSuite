             HCFR STANDARD SELECTOR

This menu shows the #CHCFR software#C profiles.

- #YREC 601 (NTSC)#Y is recommended for #CNTSC CRT#C
    users.
- #YPAL/SECAM#Y is recommended for #CPAL CRT#C users.
- #YREC 709#Y is recommended for #CHD#C and #CUHD#C in
    #CSDR#C flat panel TV users.
- #YsRGB#Y is recommended for #CPC VGA#C and up #CCRT
monitors#C. 

For #Cwestern regions#C of all color space suse #YD65#Y,
use #YD93#Y for #CJapanese SDTVs#C.
