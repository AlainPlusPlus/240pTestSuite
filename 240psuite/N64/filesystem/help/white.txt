       WHITE SCREEN (1/2)

This pattern can be changed
between white, black, red, green,
blue and custom color full
screens by using the #GR#G and #GL#G
buttons.

Pressing #GA#G while displaying the
black screen and in #CNTSC#C systems
will toggle between #Y7.5IRE#Y and
the #Y0IRE#Y below black levels.

Pressing #GA#G while displaying the
white screen will enter color
edit mode, to select the RGB color
to fill the screen with. Use the
d-pad & #GC buttons#G to change color.

Some displays and scalers have
issues when changing between a
black #Y0IRE#Y and a white screen.

                        #C(cont...)#C
       WHITE SCREEN (2/2)


This pattern can also be used to
check color purity, and for dead
pixels in some displays.
